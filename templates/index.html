Retinal Disease Classification System

Upload a retinal image to classify it as normal or identify specific eye diseases

About the Project

This web application uses a deep learning model to classify retinal images into four categories:

  • NORMAL: Healthy retina with no signs of disease
  • DIABETIC RETINOPATHY: Damage to retinal blood vessels caused by diabetes
  • CATARACT: Clouding of the eye's natural lens visible in retinal imaging
  • GLAUCOMA: Optic nerve damage often associated with increased eye pressure

The model was trained on a comprehensive dataset of retinal images to ensure accurate classification across various disease stages and image qualities, making it suitable for diverse clinical settings.
